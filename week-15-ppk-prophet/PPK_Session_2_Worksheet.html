3. True or False

Test your understanding! Tap True or False for each statement.

Jesus was only a prophet during His earthly ministry, not before His incarnation.
Correct! Jesus was the Prophet from the beginning - He spoke to Adam and Eve, called Abraham, and wrestled with Jacob before His incarnation.
Moses prophesied that God would raise up another prophet like him who must be listened to.
Correct! In Acts 3:22, Peter quotes Moses saying God would raise up a prophet like him from among the people, and everyone must listen to everything he tells them.
Jesus only reveals the truth to us; we must understand it on our own.
Correct! Jesus doesn't just reveal truth - He opens our minds to understand it. Without His help, we have "light but not sight."
