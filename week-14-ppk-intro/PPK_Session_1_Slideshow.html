Key Truth

In the person of Christ we have the fulfillment of not one but all three offices, PPK.
1
PPK is revealed in the Old Testament
God related to His people through three divinely instituted offices: prophet, priest, and king.
2
PPK is fulfilled in Christ's earthly ministry
Everything Jesus did in His earthly ministry fell into one of these three roles.
3
PPK is exemplified in Christ's heavenly ministry
Jesus was, is, and always will be Prophet, Priest, and King.
4
Jesus uniquely fulfills all three offices
He reveals the truth AND IS the truth; He offers the sacrifice AND IS the sacrifice; He rules as King AND serves as King.
5
PPK is a model for husbands and fathers
It provides a biblical pattern for how men are to spiritually lead their families.
6
The origin of PPK
After the fall, God temporarily met the need for a single Messiah through these three offices.
7
The fulfillment of PPK
Jesus was anointed at His baptism as the ultimate Prophet, Priest, and King.
8
Why we need all three equally
We can't have a partial Messiah—we need Jesus equally as Prophet, Priest, and King.
