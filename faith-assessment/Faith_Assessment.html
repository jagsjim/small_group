If this assessment reveals areas of concern, that's GOOD - it means the Holy Spirit may be working in your heart. This could reveal that you need true salvation for the first time, OR that you have a wrong understanding of what biblical Christianity actually looks like.

If you realize you need a heart change, cry out to Jesus - He loves to save!

üôè Final Reflection

Take a moment to pray and ask God to show you the truth about your spiritual condition. Remember, if you have any doubts, that's not necessarily bad - it might be God drawing you to true salvation for the first time, or to a deeper relationship with Him.

If you realize you may not be truly saved: Don't despair! God loves you and wants to save you. Confess your sin, turn from it, and trust in Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior. He died for your sins and rose again to give you eternal life.

If this confirmed your faith: Praise God! Continue to grow in grace and help others come to know the real Jesus.
