God made you to live under King Jesus, and since you will inevitably serve whatever you seek first, He wages war against false kings in your heart through His word and humbling circumstances to set you free for abundant life in Him.
1
We were created to live under the perfect rule of King Jesus, not to rule ourselves.
2
Whatever you seek after first, you will inevitably end up serving—this is part of the human condition.
3
Good things become false kings when we make them ultimate things, and false kings always disappoint.
4
Jesus rescues us by waging war against the lies and false beliefs that keep us from seeing His worth.
5
King Jesus comes with meekness and gentleness, using His word as the weapon to demolish strongholds.
6
He targets both our false arguments (wrong belief systems) and our pretensions (pride and self-will).
7
God uses humbling circumstances and trials to bring down our pride so we can see how much we need Him.
8
The kingship of Jesus in our life is directly proportional to the word of Jesus in our life.
