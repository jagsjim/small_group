üëÜ Tap to Reveal Key Truths

What does "propitiation" mean? (Tap to reveal)
Satisfying God's justice. Jesus took our judgment so God can bless us instead of judge us.
What does "expiation" mean? (Tap to reveal)
Carrying away our sin-guilt. Like the scapegoat, Jesus removes our sin completely.
How does Jesus intercede for us now? (Tap to reveal)
By His resurrected presence at God's right hand, bearing nail-pierced hands that prove "it is finished."
Why can't our salvation fail? (Tap to reveal)
Because Jesus lives forever as our High Priest. For our salvation to fail, He would have to die‚Äîbut He cannot die.
