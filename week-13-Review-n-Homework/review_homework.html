Independent Bible Study Practice

Purpose

This week, we're practicing independent Bible study. Each person will review one week's lesson, answer four questions, and share their findings with the group. This helps us learn how to study God's Word on our own while also learning from each other.

  1. Find your assigned week below - Each person has one specific week

  4. When done, click "Email My Notes" - This opens your email app with your notes ready to send to yourself
  5. Bring your notes to the next meeting - You'll share what you learned (3-5 minutes)

üé§ Presentation Guide

When You Share (3-5 minutes):

  1. Briefly summarize your week's topic - One sentence about the main idea
  2. Answer your four questions - Share what you learned from the Bible
  3. Share your supporting verses - Read them from your Bible and explain briefly how they connect
  4. Optional: Share one personal application - How will this affect your life?
Example Format:

"My week was on Forgiveness. The main idea is that the Bible teaches us to forgive others because God forgave us through Christ.

For question 1, the Bible says... [explain]
For question 2, I learned that Scripture teaches... [explain]
For question 3... [explain]
For question 4... [explain]

The verses I found were [reference] which says [read from your Bible] and [reference] which says [read]. These verses show us that...

One way I want to apply this is..."
Remember:
  • The study guide helps you understand, but quote from the Bible itself
  • Goal is to practice studying God's Word independently
  • We're learning together about the gospel
  • No pressure to be perfect - we're all growing!
